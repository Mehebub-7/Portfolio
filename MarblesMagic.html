Marbles Magic: Marbles Games

An engaging marble-shooting puzzle game where players aim to eliminate chains of colorful marbles by matching three or more of the same color.

My Role

I led the development of the dynamic level progression system and implemented a variety of power-ups to enhance gameplay strategy.

Technologies

  • Unity (URP)
  • C#
  • Cinemachine
  • Scriptable Objects
  • Node-based path for AI
